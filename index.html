✦ a poem for the woman who is my whole world ✦

You were the first song I ever heard, long before I knew what music was. Your voice, a lullaby written into my bones, your hands — the first home I ever knew. You gave me roots so I could grow, and wings so I could leave — then loved me all the more each time I dared to fly. In every comfort I reach for, in every strength I somehow find, in every soft and certain place — Mama, you are there. No page is wide enough, no words are true enough, to hold what you mean to me. But know this, always — you are the poem my life keeps trying to write. 🌸
— written with love, for you 💙
